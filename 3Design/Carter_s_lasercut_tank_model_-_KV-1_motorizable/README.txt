# Summary

This is my masterpiece !

it has a scale reaching about 1:18 (~37,5 cm long).

A lasercut KV-1 that has a hollow space at the rear to put some motors for the rearwheel drive. I designed it to fit with some Opitec motor kits, with a NodeMCU to control it.

The red pathes are cut-engraved (fast and not powerful) and the black pathes are cut (slow and powerful).

See also my <a href="https://www.thingiverse.com/thing:2560738">StuG III Ausf. F</a>, my <a href="https://www.thingiverse.com/thing:2731341">Sherman</a> or my <a href="https://www.thingiverse.com/thing:2761024">B1 bis</a>
